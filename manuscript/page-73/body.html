On looking it up, I find that she was right. We had mutton stew and pie-plant for lunch--hate 'em both; they taste like the asylum. The post brought me nothing but bills (though I must say that I never do get anything else; my family are not the kind that write). In English class this afternoon we had an unexpected written lesson. This was it:

           I asked no other thing,
           No other was denied.
           I offered Being for it;
           The mighty merchant smiled.

           Brazil?  He twirled a button
